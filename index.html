Our Services

In our studios you can learn to paint and build sculptures. Here you can learn different art styles and methods together with our students. Our experienced and professional art teachers accompany you through your journey and help you to improve step by step. We have 4 work rooms where our participants can work together in different groups or alone.

About Us

Our instructors are not only skilled in visual arts but also experienced in fields like philosophy, psychology, and social coaching. This helps maintain a lively and suitable atmosphere in our classes. Each person has their own unique talents and characteristics and can achieve the best growth under specific conditions. This can be somewhat compared to gardening. We do not have specific age restrictions in our classes, but before starting, we will give you a test to evaluate your talents and personal characteristics. Our instructors will then recommend the appropriate class and group for you.
